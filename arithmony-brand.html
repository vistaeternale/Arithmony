01
Brand Concept

The precision of math.
The grace of balance.

Arithmony is the meeting point of two forces: the exacting rigor of mathematics and the human desire for peace. Every line is intentional. Every number has a place. The brand reflects what good budgeting actually feels like — not anxiety and restraint, but structure, clarity, and the quiet confidence of knowing exactly where you stand.

01.1
Structured Harmony

Order isn't oppressive — it's liberating. Arithmony uses geometric precision as a symbol of financial clarity. Art deco influences bring warmth to structure, turning spreadsheets into architecture.

01.2
Editorial Confidence

Arithmony speaks with authority, not anxiety. The brand is aspirational but grounded — like a financial publication that respects your intelligence rather than simplifying everything for you.

01.3
Growth as Aesthetic

Emerald isn't an accent color — it's a worldview. Growth, vitality, momentum. Used sparingly, it signals progress without triumphalism. Every chart tick upward, every saved dollar, earns its green.

01.4
Mathematical Elegance

The name itself — Arithmony — fuses arithmetic with harmony. The identity reflects this compound nature: precise mono type for figures, classical serifs for warmth, geometric forms for structure.

01.5
Ivory over White

Pure white is clinical. Arithmony's surfaces are warm ivory — the color of fine paper, of a document that matters. It says this is considered, not mass-produced. Intentional, not default.

01.6
Navy as Anchor

Deep navy grounds everything. It carries the weight of institutional trust — banks, law firms, heritage brands — while remaining approachable alongside ivory and emerald. It's authority without arrogance.

04
Logo System

A mark built from
the letter itself.

The Arithmony logo mark is a geometric abstraction of the letter A — formed by a diamond frame, two rising legs, and a precise crossbar. It references balance scales, architectural forms, and the peak of a well-constructed budget. It works at any scale.

Clear Space

Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the letter "A" on all sides. Never crowd the mark.

Minimum Size

Symbol mark: 24px minimum. Full lockup: 140px wide minimum. Below these thresholds, use wordmark only.

Never Do

No color modifications. No stretching. No drop shadows. No gradient fills on the symbol mark. No decorative borders around the lockup.

07
Voice & Tone

How Arithmony speaks.

The brand voice is that of a knowledgeable, trusted advisor who respects your intelligence. Precise without being cold. Encouraging without being saccharine. Mathematical concepts expressed with genuine warmth.

Confident & Precise

"Your grocery spending is 18% above your three-month average. Here's what changed."

We state facts clearly. We don't hedge. We don't say "it looks like" or "it seems." We've done the math and we say what it shows.

Warm, Not Cheerful

"You're $240 ahead of where you were last March. That's real progress."

We acknowledge milestones with genuine warmth, not exclamation points. Financial improvement is meaningful — it deserves a measured, sincere response.

Structural Clarity

"Three categories are over budget. Two are recoverable this month."

We organize information like a well-designed ledger. Signal before detail. Category before line item. The big picture before the footnote.

Never Shame, Always Frame

"Dining out accounted for 31% of discretionary spending — which leaves $180 of your fun budget flexible."

We describe, we never judge. Every figure is context, not verdict. The user is always intelligent, always capable, always in control of their own story.
