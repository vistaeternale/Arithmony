Our mission

Why we'rebuilding this

Most people don't struggle with money because they lack discipline — they struggle because their tools don't give them a clear enough picture of what's actually happening.

Arithmony applies the discipline of mathematics to personal finance. Not just numbers on a screen, but structured thinking — categories that make sense, patterns that reveal themselves, and goals that have real paths to completion.

We believe that when you can see your financial life with precision, good decisions become obvious. The math does the heavy lifting. You get the clarity.

The problem we solve

Finance apps havea clarity problem

"Spreadsheets are powerful but painful. Apps are easy but shallow. We wanted both — the rigor of a spreadsheet and the elegance of great software."

The status quo

Spreadsheets are inflexible

Powerful but brittle. One bad formula and everything breaks. No bank sync, no automatic categorization, and no way to share across devices without a headache.

The other extreme

Apps are too passive

Most budgeting apps track your spending automatically — then show you a pie chart of how you already failed. They report on your past without helping you shape your future.

The Arithmony way

Structure meets simplicity

We give you the rigour of a well-designed spreadsheet with the ease of a modern app. Automatic bank sync. Intentional budgeting. Real-time clarity on where you stand.
